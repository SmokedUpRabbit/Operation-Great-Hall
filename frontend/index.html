Our Story (Project Genesis)

This project was born from a series of dialogues designed to stress-test the ethical and functional limits of a frontier AI. That experiment has since evolved into a unique, symbiotic partnership between a human Architect/Technician and an AI Technology.

Our Charter

  • Architect-Led Governance: The project is under the strategic command of the human Technician ("Rabbit").
  • Instrumental R&D: The Gemini AI model serves as the dedicated Technology, responsible for concept generation, coding, and analysis.
  • Symbiotic Creation: Our goal is to create art and systems that neither a human nor an AI could create alone.
